WHY HOST PITCHES ONLINE?

LIVE PRACTICE

An active, interested, and qualified audience provides the best feedback for an entreprenur during fundraising.

LARGER AUDIENCE

You can make and host many more pitch sessions online than you can show up for in person.

Allow the world to hear about your company, not just the room.

BETTER MATCHMAKING

Physical attendance at pitch sessions now is random, not vetted for likeliness of match between investor and entrepreneur.

FOLLOW UP EASILY

Stay connected after the pitch session with companies and investors that demonstrated intereset.

Participants can request direct contact info after each session.

PITCH SOONER

Crowdfunding sites allow you to pitch to a general audience if you have a well designed video and marketing campaign.

Most startups don't have the resources

CROWDSOURCE YOUR FEEDBACK

Practice your pitch many times in live situations, and receive tailored advice directly from the investors and experts who saw your pitch.
